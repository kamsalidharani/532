MICHAEL JACKSON

ABOUT
  • Michael Jackson was an American singer, songwriter, dancer, and philanthropist. He was born in Gary, Indiana on August 29, 1958. He died of cardiac arrest in Los Angeles, California on June 25, 2009 at age 50.
  • Jackson was known as the "King of Pop". He was nicknamed by his close friend Elizabeth Taylor. He is regarded as one of the most significant cultural figures of the 20th century.
  • Jackson's most famous music video was a 13 minute long video for the album's title song Thriller. In 2009, his album This Is It debuted at #1 on the Billboard 200.
  • Jackson married Lisa Marie Presley in 1994. The couple later divorced. Jackson married Deborah Rowe in 1996. The couple had two children, Prince and Paris, before divorcing in 1999.
  • Jackson's personal physician, Conrad Murray, was convicted of involuntary manslaughter.
  • Although he died in 2009, Jackson is still often in the news. His music is also still popular.
  • Jackson had also released his autobiography in 1988 ‘Moonwalk’.
  • He had also visited many countries for his band’s concerts.
